Contrary - a cozy indie hand drawn art like game in a website you open the website choose a mathamatical paradox to play it redirects and the game/canvas loads a visual novel styled simulation of the paradox which is interactive (art+music+sound effects) then say you're playing the monty hall problem the host comes you choose a door etc and then in the end the Math is revealed with stats and graphs.

Theme - light warm color sketch book 
assets - from the internet of hand drawn (mspaint gng time to show my art skills)

pages - three or more paradoxes, landing page thats it more in future

features - a landing page with CTA button a page to choose paradoxes background music volume slider, sound effects, art and math used shown in end with explanation and references link

font - a handwriting font a cute font rounded corners 

elements - will draw manually buttons etc and background cards etc in assets
