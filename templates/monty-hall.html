Why Switching Wins

When you first pick, you have a 1/3 chance of choosing the car and 2/3 chance of choosing a goat. The host ALWAYS reveals a goat from the remaining doors.

If you picked a goat initially (2/3 chance): The car is behind the other unopened door. Switching wins!

If you picked the car initially (1/3 chance): Switching loses.

That's why switching gives you 2/3 (66.7%) chance of winning!
